The 2011 Airship Victoria Soundtrack. This is a studio version of the live set played at Burning Man as part of the Airship Victoria project.

All of this music was originally written with a nine foot tall Tesla coil in mind as its primary lead synth. Due to logistical issues obtaining and recording with this same coil after the burn, the songs have been reworked to incorporate new sounds for the lead synth parts.
